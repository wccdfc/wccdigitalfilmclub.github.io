About Us

WCC Digital Film Club is a community where dedicated filmmakers get together to create inspiring films regardless of their backgrounds. We ecourage our members who love script writting to allow us help their visions come a reality. In our meetings we do discussions to hands-on work workshops. Feel free to visit us!

Our mission

The Digital Film Club aims to provide a safe environment for creative minds to express themselves. We not only strive on producing films that represent diversity, including the LGBTQIA+ community, women, people of color, and more. But to grow our community where all minds matters and we empower each other through collaboration and unity.

Through our members efforts and a dedication to the film making process, we will create art that will impact the future and shape it into a better industry where we welcome the next generations.
